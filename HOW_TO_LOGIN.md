# 🔐 How to Login - Zuba House App

## 📱 **HOW TO ACCESS LOGIN SCREEN**

### **Method 1: From Home Screen**
1. Open the app (it starts on Home screen)
2. Tap the **"Account"** tab at the bottom (person icon)
3. If not logged in, you'll see a login prompt
4. Tap **"Login"** or **"Sign In"** button

### **Method 2: When Adding to Cart**
1. Browse products
2. Tap **"Add to Cart"**
3. If not logged in, a popup will appear
4. Tap **"Login"** button in the popup

### **Method 3: Direct Navigation**
- The app allows guest browsing
- Login is only required for:
  - Adding items to cart
  - Viewing orders
  - Managing wishlist
  - Checkout

---

## ✅ **LOGIN STEPS**

1. **Open Login Screen** (see methods above)

2. **Enter Credentials:**
   - **Email:** Your registered email address
   - **Password:** Your account password

3. **Tap "Sign In"** button

4. **Wait for authentication** (usually 1-2 seconds)

5. **You'll be automatically logged in** and redirected to Home

---

## 🆕 **DON'T HAVE AN ACCOUNT?**

### **Register New Account:**

1. **From Login Screen:**
   - Tap **"Don't have an account? Sign up"** link
   - OR tap **"Create Account"** button

2. **Fill Registration Form:**
   - **Full Name:** Your name
   - **Email:** Your email address
   - **Password:** At least 6 characters
   - **Confirm Password:** Re-enter password

3. **Tap "Sign Up"** button

4. **You'll be automatically logged in** after registration

---

## 🔑 **LOGIN OPTIONS**

### **1. Email/Password Login**
- Enter your email and password
- Tap "Sign In"

### **2. Google Login** (if available)
- Tap "Continue with Google" button
- Select your Google account
- Automatically logged in

### **3. Forgot Password?**
- Tap "Forgot Password?" link on login screen
- Enter your email
- Check email for OTP code
- Enter OTP and reset password

## ❓ **TROUBLESHOOTING**

### **"Invalid email or password"**
- Check email spelling
- Check password (case-sensitive)
- Try "Forgot Password" to reset

### **"User not found"**
- You need to register first
- Tap "Sign Up" to create account

### **"Network error"**
- Check internet connection
- Make sure backend API is running
- API URL: `https://zuba-api.onrender.com`

### **Can't see Login button?**
- App allows guest browsing
- Login only needed for:
  - Adding to cart
  - Checkout
  - Viewing orders
